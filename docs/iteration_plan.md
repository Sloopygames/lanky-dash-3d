### Iteration Plan Steps
1. **Review Design Documents**: Analyze the game design documents to identify key areas for improvement.
2. **Identify Incremental Changes**: List specific changes, including:
   - Emotionally charged narrative arcs with branching paths.
   - Dynamic environment interactions that respond to player actions.
   - Asymmetrical gameplay roles in multiplayer modes.
3. **Prioritize Changes**: Rank changes based on impact and feasibility, focusing on low-risk adjustments first.
4. **Implement Changes**: Begin coding and integrating prioritized changes into the game.
5. **Test Changes**: Conduct internal testing to identify any issues arising from the new changes.
6. **Gather Feedback**: Involve a small group of players to test the changes and provide feedback.
7. **Iterate Based on Feedback**: Make necessary adjustments based on player feedback and testing results.
8. **Prepare for Governance Audit**: Document all changes made and prepare for a governance audit to ensure compliance with project standards.
